Common Questions

Can I switch trainers if needed?

Yes. If your goals change or you prefer a different training style, we can match you with another trainer. Just let us know.

Do I need to be a member first?

Yes, personal training is available to all Echelon Fits members. You can purchase a single session to try it out, but ongoing packages require an active membership.

What if I'm brand new to working out?

Perfect! Personal training is ideal for beginners. Your trainer will start where you are, teach you everything you need to know, and build your confidence gradually.

Can I share sessions with a workout partner?

Yes, partner training is available at a discounted rate per person. Both partners split the session time and work on compatible goals. Ask about pricing when booking.

How long are the sessions?

All personal training sessions are 60 minutes. This includes warm-up, workout, cool-down, and any questions or form checks.

Do sessions roll over if I don't use them?

Sessions must be used within the month. Unused sessions don't roll over to the next month, so book regularly to get the most value from your package.
