How to Choose

Choose Basic if you:

  • • Work out during regular daytime hours
  • • Prefer independent training without classes
  • • Want to try gym membership without commitment

Choose Standard if you:

  • • Need flexible hours including early morning or late night
  • • Want unlimited access to group fitness classes
  • • Like the convenience of towel service

Choose Premium if you:

  • • Want personal training twice a month
  • • Bring guests regularly (up to 5 per month)
  • • Value nutrition guidance and progress tracking

Choose VIP if you:

  • • Train seriously with 2x weekly personal training
  • • Want luxury amenities and priority access
  • • Appreciate recovery services like massage therapy

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch membership levels?

Yes. You can upgrade anytime and the change takes effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. Contact the front desk or email us to make changes.

Do you offer student or military discounts?

Yes. Students with valid ID and active military members receive 15% off any membership level. Bring proof of eligibility when you sign up.

What if I need to pause my membership?

Standard and higher memberships can freeze for up to 3 months per year at $10/month. Basic memberships can freeze for 1 month per year. Freezes must be requested at least 7 days before your next billing date.

How do guest passes work?

Guest passes allow you to bring a friend who isn't a member. Passes reset monthly and don't roll over. Guests must be 18+ and sign a waiver at the front desk. They get full gym access during your visit.

Are personal training sessions included or extra?

Premium and VIP memberships include monthly personal training sessions as listed. Additional sessions can be purchased separately. Basic and Standard members can buy personal training packages—see our personal training page for pricing.

What classes are included with Standard+?

All group fitness classes: HIIT, yoga, spin, strength training, boxing, and recovery sessions. Check the live schedule in our mobile app or on the classes page. Most classes are 45-60 minutes and require advance booking.

Can I try before I commit?

Yes. We offer a free 3-day trial pass for first-time visitors. No credit card required. Visit during staffed hours (Mon-Fri 6AM-10PM) to get your trial pass and tour the facility.

How do I cancel if needed?

Submit a cancellation request via email or at the front desk with 30 days notice. You'll have access through the end of your paid billing cycle. No cancellation fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and ACH bank transfers for monthly billing. Payment is processed on the same date each month. No cash or check payments for recurring memberships.

Is there parking available?

Yes. Free parking lot with 50+ spaces for all members. VIP members get reserved spots near the entrance. Parking is well-lit and monitored by security cameras 24/7.
